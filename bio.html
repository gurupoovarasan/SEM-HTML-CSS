Lion is always lion

Thala For A Reson

MS Dhoni’s cricket career is marked by numerous achievements and milestones. He made his ODI debut in 2004 and quickly rose to prominence. As captain, he led India to victories in the 2007 ICC T20 World Cup and the 2011 ICC Cricket World Cup, becoming the first captain to win all three ICC limited-overs trophies. Dhoni is renowned for his finishing skills, holding a highest ODI score of 183 and over 10,000 runs. Dhoni retired from international cricket in 2020, leaving an indelible legacy.

In addition to his international success, MS Dhoni has had a stellar IPL career with the Chennai Super Kings, leading them to multiple championships. His strategic mindset, calmness in high-pressure situations, and sportsmanship have made him a revered figure in cricket worldwide.
