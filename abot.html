THALA

Mahendra Singh Dhoni, often referred to as MS Dhoni, is a legendary Indian cricketer and former captain of the Indian national team. Born on July 7, 1981, in Ranchi, he is renowned for his calm demeanor and exceptional leadership skills. Dhoni led India to victory in the ICC T20 World Cup in 2007 and the ICC Cricket World Cup in 2011, making him a national hero. Known for his finishing ability and innovative wicketkeeping, he has amassed numerous records, including being the only captain to win all three ICC limited-overs trophies. Dhoni retired from international cricket in 2020 but continues to play in the Indian Premier League.

MS Dhoni is celebrated for his strategic acumen and remarkable composure under pressure. He made his debut in 2004 and quickly became a key player. His iconic helicopter shot is famous among fans. Beyond cricket, Dhoni is an entrepreneur and a fitness enthusiast, inspiring millions with his dedication and sportsmanship.
